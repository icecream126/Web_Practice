BCI란 무엇인가?

//소제목

A brain-computer interface(BCI), sometimes called a neural-control interface(NCI), is a direct communication pathway between an enhanced or wired brain and an external device.

//이미지 추가

BCI differs from neuromodulation in that it allows for biodirectional information flow. BCIs are often directed at researching, mapping, assisting, augmenting, or repairing human cognitive or sensory-motor functions.
